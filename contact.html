Quick Questions

Is the initial consultation really free? +
Yes, absolutely. The first consultation is 100% free. We'll assess your profile, discuss your options, and give you honest advice — no strings attached. We only charge service fees if you decide to proceed with us after the consultation.
How soon can I speak to an advisor? +
For WhatsApp inquiries, we typically respond within 1–2 hours during office hours (Mon–Sat, 9 AM–6 PM). For form submissions, we call back within 2 hours. For urgent matters, WhatsApp is always the fastest way to reach us.
Can students from outside Jaffna contact you? +
Absolutely! While we're based in Jaffna, we serve students from across Sri Lanka — Colombo, Kandy, Vavuniya, Galle, and everywhere else. All consultations can be done online via WhatsApp video call or Zoom. Many of our successful students came from Colombo and other cities.
