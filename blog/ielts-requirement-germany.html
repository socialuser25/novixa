IELTS Requirements for Germany — Can You Study Without IELTS?

TestDaF, DSH, and IELTS alternatives explained for Sri Lankan students going to Germany.

📅 February 2026 ✍️ Navixa Education ⏱️ 5 min read

One of the most common questions we get from Sri Lankan students is: "Do I need IELTS to study in Germany?" The answer might surprise you — it depends entirely on which program and university you choose.

The Two Language Paths in Germany

German universities offer programs in two languages: German and English. Your language requirement depends on which type of program you choose:

Path 1: German-Taught Programs (Most Programs)

Germany has thousands of German-taught degree programs — the vast majority of Bachelor's programs and many Master's programs are taught in German. For these, you need:

  • TestDaF Level 4 (TDN 4): The most internationally recognized German proficiency test. Available in Sri Lanka through the Goethe-Institut Colombo.
  • DSH-2 (Deutsche Sprachprüfung für den Hochschulzugang): University-specific language test, usually taken after completing a preparatory language course in Germany.
  • Goethe-Zertifikat C1: Accepted by many universities as an alternative to TestDaF.

No IELTS needed for German-taught programs. But you need German proficiency instead!

Path 2: English-Taught Programs (Growing Fast)

Germany now has hundreds of English-taught programs, especially at Master's level. These typically require:

  • IELTS Academic: Usually 6.0–6.5 overall (with minimum 5.5–6.0 in each band)
  • TOEFL iBT: Usually 80–90+
  • PTE Academic: Some universities accept this as an alternative
  • Duolingo English Test: Accepted by a growing number of universities

💡 Good news for Sri Lankan students: Many German universities accept a Medium of Instruction (MOI) certificate instead of IELTS — if your Bachelor's degree was taught in English. This means no IELTS needed at all for many students!

Can I Study in Germany WITHOUT IELTS?

Yes! Here are the three scenarios where Sri Lankan students can study in Germany without IELTS:

  • 1. Choose a German-taught program: No IELTS needed at all. You need TestDaF 4 or DSH-2 in German instead. Note: This requires learning German first (12–18 months from scratch).
  • 2. Use your Medium of Instruction certificate: If your degree was taught in English (most Sri Lankan university degrees are), many German universities accept a signed letter from your university confirming the medium of instruction. This replaces IELTS entirely at those universities.
  • 3. Previous English education: Some universities accept O/L or A/L results from English-medium schools as proof of English proficiency.

Recommended Language Learning Timeline for Sri Lankans

A1

German A1 — 3 months

Basics: greetings, numbers, simple sentences. Start online at Deutsche Welle (free) or Goethe-Institut Colombo.

B1

German B1 — 9–12 months

Conversational German. Can handle most everyday situations. Required for language preparation courses in Germany.

C1

German C1 / TestDaF 4 — 18–24 months

University-level German. Can understand academic lectures and write scholarly essays. Required for admission to German-taught university programs.

Best English-Taught Programs in Germany for Sri Lankans

  • Engineering: TU Munich (MS Robotics, Informatics), RWTH Aachen (MS Mechanical), KIT (MS Computer Science)
  • Business: Mannheim Business School, Frankfurt School of Finance
  • Computer Science: TU Berlin, TU Darmstadt, University of Stuttgart
  • Data Science/AI: TU Munich, University of Hamburg, Saarland University
