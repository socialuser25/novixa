About Navixa Education

Sri Lanka's trusted European education consultancy — based in Jaffna, serving students across the island since 2004.

🎓
Our Story

Born from a Passion to Change Lives

Navixa Education was founded with one mission: make quality European education accessible to every Sri Lankan student, regardless of which city they're from.

We saw too many talented students from Jaffna, Vavuniya, and the North give up on their dreams simply because they didn't know where to start. We changed that.

Today, with 150+ alumni studying in Germany, UK, Italy, and across Europe, we are the most trusted name in study abroad consultancy in Northern Sri Lanka.

20+ years combined expertise in European education
Based in Chavakacheri, Jaffna — serving all of Sri Lanka
98% visa approval rate across all destinations
No hidden fees — transparent pricing always
Personal advisor assigned to every student
